Allstate

Transformative Growth

Prior to Transformative Growth, Allstate's traditional insurance model relied only on agents to service its customers either in-person or via phone. If a customer were to use one of Allstate’s digital products, the user would still need the assistance of a human. Transformative Growth is a new insurance model that provides a fully self-service digital experience without having to go through an agent. With this new digital platform, Allstate looks to increase market share and stay ahead of its competitors by reducing costs, expanding customer access, and investing in new technology.

I joined the Transformative Growth team in the beginning of 2020. My responsibilities focused on information architecture, interaction design, prototyping, usability testing, and some visual design. I worked on multiple aspects of this new product from policy changes to payments. This specific case study focuses on how an existing Allstate customer would make a vehicle change to their auto policy. The tools I used included Sketch, Sketch Cloud, and Axure RP.
